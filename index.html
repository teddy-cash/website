TSD: Algorithmic USD Stablecoin

TSD is minted when users deposit AVAX as collateral and open troves. The mechanism for its price stability is described in detail in the excellent Liquity docs.

TEDDY: Growth Token

Stake TEDDYs and earn TSD everytime someone opens a trove. Earn AVAX everytime someone redeems TSD. Governance token for future versions of the protocol (the current smart contracts are immutable).

A Decentralized & Immutable Protocol

Teddy Cash works without governance. The smart contracts are immutable. No central UI necessary.
